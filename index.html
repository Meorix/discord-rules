Community Guidelines

Welcome to our community! Please take a moment to review and agree to our guidelines for a great experience for everyone.

Be Respectful

▼

Treat everyone with kindness and respect. We have a zero-tolerance policy for harassment, hate speech, and personal attacks. This includes insults, slurs, and discrimination based on any personal attribute.

Keep it Friendly

▼

This is a place for fun and good vibes! Please avoid excessive negativity, drama, or spamming. If you have a disagreement, try to resolve it calmly and privately.

No NSFW Content

▼

Keep all content safe for work (SFW). This means no explicit, graphically violent, or inappropriate images, videos, or links. We want this to be a safe space for all members.

Don't Spam

▼

Avoid flooding channels with the same message, images, or links. This includes excessive emojis, memes, or bot commands.

No Self-Promotion

▼

Please don't advertise your own server, products, or social media channels without permission from an admin. If you want to share something, ask an admin first!

Follow Discord's ToS

▼

You must follow all of Discord's own Terms of Service. If you violate their rules, you will also be removed from our server.

Consequences

Minor Offenses

Minor offenses will result in a warning from our moderation team.

Serious/Repeated Offenses

Repeated or serious offenses will lead to a temporary or permanent ban from the server.

If you have any questions or see someone breaking the rules, please contact a moderator or admin.

Thanks for being a part of our community! We're glad you're here!
